Kadin Codes

kadin.codes is less a blog and more a long-running notebook.

Some entries are technical. Some are half-finished thoughts. Some exist only because they felt worth keeping at the time. The common thread is that everything here marks a moment: something learned, something built, something noticed, or something that refused to leave the head.

This site is not optimized for teaching, branding, or consistency. It is an archive of curiosity and process — code experiments next to personal reflections, old projects next to newer questions. Posts may be practical, speculative, nostalgic, or simply incomplete.


Nothing here is guaranteed to be polished. Some things may be wrong later. That is part of the record.

If there is a theme, it is this: keeping track of thinking over time.
